Privacy Policy for Ideas Keeper V2

Last Updated: December 23, 2024
Effective Date: December 23, 2024

1. Introduction

Ideas Keeper V2 ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application Ideas Keeper V2 (the "App").

Key Privacy Principle: Ideas Keeper V2 is designed with privacy-first architecture. All your data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Ideas Keeper V2 is built with a privacy-by-design approach. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Task or note content
  • Photos or voice recordings you add to items
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Device information or identifiers
  • Location data
  • Cookies or tracking data

3. How Your Data is Stored

All data created in Ideas Keeper V2 is stored exclusively on your device using:

  • Local Storage: Your tasks, notes, and preferences are saved in your device's local storage
  • Local File System: Photos and voice recordings are stored locally on your device
  • No Cloud Sync: No data is synchronized to cloud services
  • No Remote Servers: No data is sent to external servers

4. Data Access and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Full Ownership: All data belongs to you and remains on your device
  • Delete Anytime: You can delete individual items or all data through the app
  • Uninstall Removal: Uninstalling the app removes all associated data
  • No Account Required: No registration or account creation needed

5. Permissions

Ideas Keeper V2 may request the following device permissions:

  • Camera: Only to capture photos for your items (photos stay on device)
  • Microphone: Only to record voice notes (recordings stay on device)
  • Storage: Only to save your data locally on your device

All permissions are used solely for app functionality and no data is transmitted externally.

6. Third-Party Services

Ideas Keeper V2 does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The app operates completely independently without external connections.

7. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device:

  • Your data security depends on your device's security settings
  • We recommend using device lock screens and encryption
  • No data transmission means no interception risks
  • No server breaches possible since we don't use servers

8. Children's Privacy

Ideas Keeper V2 does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for all ages as no data collection occurs.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted here with an updated "Last Updated" date. Since no data is collected, changes will primarily be clarifications to our privacy practices.

10. International Users

Ideas Keeper V2 is designed to comply with privacy regulations worldwide including GDPR, CCPA, and others, as we do not collect or process any personal data.

11. Open Source Commitment

Ideas Keeper V2's privacy-first approach can be verified as our code practices demonstrate local-only data handling with no external network requests for user data.
